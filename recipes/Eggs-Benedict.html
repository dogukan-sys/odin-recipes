Eggs Benedict

A classic breakfast dish, this perfectly poached egg and glistening hollandaise sauce.
With simple and inexpensive ingredients that you could find in your fridge at this very moment you have no excuse not to make this.

Ingredients

  • Ground Chive
  • Hollandaise Sauce
  • Poached Egg
  • Oven Baked Bacon
  • English Muffin
  • Karasumi Powder

Assemble

  1. Cut English Muffins in half and place into a pan on low.
    Flip every 5-10 minutes until the'yre browned to your liking.
  2. Now put one Muffin at the Bottom and add ur Bacon.
  3. Put ur Poached Eggs ontop of the Bacon.
  4. Cover with as much Hollondaise as you like.
  5. Garnish with some Chives or a little Basil
